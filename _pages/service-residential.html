    Structural engineering in residential construction involves evaluating, planning, and designing structural components such foundations, walls, lateral force resisting systems, and floor/roofing systems to ensure the strength and durability needed to resist external forces from gravity loads and other environmental factors. The goal is to build with safety, stability, and longevity in mind. Our skilled structural engineers at Southeast Engineers assess the unique requirements of each residential project, considering factors like soil conditions, architectural design, local building codes, and the intended usage to determine the right application for your project.

    Our expert team of engineers excel in a range of residential structural systems, including:

    • Foundation Design
    • Retaining Structures
    • Hot-Rolled Structural Steel Structures
    • Cold-Formed Structural Steel Structures
    • Concrete Structures
    • Masonry Structures
    • Timber Structures

    We collaborate closely with architects, plan designers, contractors, and developers to create safe and efficient designs that meet both aesthetic and functional requirements. From ensuring the stability of a single-family home to constructing multi-story apartment buildings, our structural engineers can help to turn architectural visions into safe and stable living spaces for homeowners and their families.
